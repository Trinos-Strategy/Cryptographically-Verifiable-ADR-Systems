## Responsible Disclosure

Please follow these guidelines:
1. Do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until we confirm a patch or mitigation.  
2. Do not attempt to exploit the issue beyond what is necessary for proof of concept.  
3. We may credit you in the changelog or release notes if you wish.
